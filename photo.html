Traditional croissants



INGREDIENTS

For the dough:

1 kg flour
420 g water
50 g eggs
45 g fresh yeast
18 g salt
100 g granulated sugar
20 g honey
70 g butter
400 g dry butter

For the egg wash:

300 g egg yolks
30 g heavy whipping cream



Oat croissants



INGREDIENTS

1 egg
70 grams (3/4 cup) oat flour
30 grams (2 1/2 tbsp) all purposes flour
17 grams (1 tbsp) honey or maple syrup
10 grams (1 heaping tsp) coconut oil (melted)
1 tsp baking powder
pinch salt
nondairy milk (only if needed*)

Filling

1 1/2 tbsp strawberry jam (or preferred jam, cream or chocolate)
